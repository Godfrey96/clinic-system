About Us

A clinic appointment booking system, that allows patients to book an appointment to go to the public clinics around Johannesburg and only be required to go to the clinic at their appointed time with the doctor. The reason of this system is to reduce the waiting in long queues, reduce waiting patient time and over crowding at clinics to only be attended to hours later. Feeling sick? Login in and book your appointment with our app and only go to clinic to be attended.
